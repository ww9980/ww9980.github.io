#SINR 3570 SINR 3570 is a novel Siloxane-based, photo-patternable packaging material developed by Shin-Etsu MicroSi. It is known to be a good wafer bonding material and can be used for flattening dielectric layer in MEMS applications. Like SU-8 and BCB (bencocyclobutene), this material allows adhesive bonding which features low bonding temperature, ability to join different wafer materials, and high tolerance to surface topography. SINR 3570 series is based on 3170 series thus have a similar property. It's negative tone, and can easily achieve 1~25um thickness. A SINR3570-PF20's nominal (i.e. coated at 5k rpm) thickness is 20um.
